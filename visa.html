China Visa Information

Visa Types

Tourist Visa (L)

For travelers visiting China for tourism purposes. Valid for 30-90 days.

Business Visa (M)

For business-related activities. Requires invitation letter.

Transit Visa (G)

For travelers transiting through China (usually 24-144 hours).

10-Year Visa

Available for citizens of certain countries (USA, Canada, etc.).

Required Documents

  • Passport valid for at least 6 months with blank pages
  • Completed visa application form
  • Recent passport-sized photo (33mm x 48mm)
  • Proof of travel itinerary (flight reservations)
  • Hotel bookings or invitation letter
  • For business visa: invitation letter from Chinese company

Application Process

1

Complete Application Form

Fill out the online form on the Chinese visa application website.

2

Prepare Documents

Gather all required documents as listed above.

3

Submit Application

Submit in person at a Chinese embassy/consulate or authorized visa center.

4

Pay Fees

Visa fees vary by nationality and processing time (standard/express).

5

Collect Visa

Processing typically takes 4-5 business days (express available).
