China's Must-Visit Destinations

Beijing

China's capital with 3,000 years of history and modern marvels.

Top Attractions

  • Forbidden City
  • Great Wall of China
  • Temple of Heaven
  • Summer Palace

Must-Try Foods

  • Peking Duck
  • Zhajiangmian (Noodles with Fried Sauce)
  • Beijing Yogurt

Travel Tips

  • Visit the Great Wall at Mutianyu for fewer crowds
  • Avoid summer months due to heat and crowds
  • Purchase attraction tickets online in advance

Shanghai

China's most cosmopolitan city blending East and West.

Top Attractions

  • The Bund
  • Yu Garden
  • Shanghai Tower
  • Tianzifang

Must-Try Foods

  • Xiaolongbao (Soup Dumplings)
  • Shengjianbao (Pan-Fried Buns)
  • Shanghai Hairy Crab

Travel Tips

  • Visit the Bund at night for spectacular lights
  • Use the metro - it's efficient and inexpensive
  • Explore the French Concession for cafes and boutiques

Xi'an

Ancient capital and starting point of the Silk Road.

Top Attractions

  • Terracotta Warriors
  • Ancient City Wall
  • Muslim Quarter
  • Big Wild Goose Pagoda

Must-Try Foods

  • Roujiamo (Chinese Hamburger)
  • Yangrou Paomo (Lamb Soup with Bread)
  • Biángbiáng Noodles

Travel Tips

  • Hire an English-speaking guide for the Terracotta Warriors
  • Visit the Muslim Quarter in the evening for food stalls
  • Spring and autumn have the most pleasant weather

Chengdu

Home of pandas and Sichuan cuisine.

Top Attractions

  • Chengdu Research Base of Giant Panda Breeding
  • Jinli Ancient Street
  • Wuhou Shrine
  • Qingcheng Mountain

Must-Try Foods

  • Hot Pot
  • Mapo Tofu
  • Dandan Noodles

Travel Tips

  • Visit pandas early morning when they're most active
  • Ask for "wei la" (less spicy) if you can't handle heat
  • Tea houses are great for people watching

Guilin

Stunning karst landscapes along the Li River.

Top Attractions

  • Li River Cruise
  • Yangshuo
  • Elephant Trunk Hill
  • Longji Rice Terraces

Must-Try Foods

  • Guilin Rice Noodles
  • Beer Fish
  • Stuffed Li River Snails

Travel Tips

  • Take the Li River cruise from Guilin to Yangshuo
  • Rent a bike in Yangshuo to explore countryside
  • April-May and September-October have best weather

Hong Kong

Vibrant metropolis with stunning skyline and delicious dim sum.

Top Attractions

  • Victoria Peak
  • Star Ferry
  • Tian Tan Buddha
  • Temple Street Night Market

Must-Try Foods

  • Dim Sum
  • Egg Waffles
  • Roast Goose

Travel Tips

  • Get an Octopus card for public transport
  • Visit Victoria Peak on a clear day
  • Try dai pai dong (open-air food stalls)

Hangzhou

Paradise on Earth with lakes, tea fields, and temples.

Top Attractions

  • West Lake
  • Lingyin Temple
  • Longjing Tea Plantations
  • Hefang Street

Must-Try Foods

  • Dongpo Pork
  • West Lake Fish in Vinegar Sauce
  • Beggar's Chicken

Travel Tips

  • Visit early morning to avoid tourist crowds
  • Rent a boat on West Lake
  • Sample Longjing tea with locals

Suzhou

Classical gardens and ancient silk culture.

Top Attractions

  • Humble Administrator's Garden
  • Tiger Hill
  • Suzhou Museum
  • Grand Canal

Must-Try Foods

  • Squirrel-Shaped Mandarin Fish
  • Suzhou-style Mooncakes
  • Sweet and Sour Spare Ribs

Travel Tips

  • Avoid weekends for garden visits
  • Combine with Hangzhou for a short trip
  • Suzhou Rail Transit connects most attractions

Nanjing

Historic capital with rich cultural sites.

Top Attractions

  • Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum
  • Nanjing City Wall
  • Confucius Temple
  • Presidential Palace

Must-Try Foods

  • Salted Duck
  • Duck Blood Noodle Soup
  • Nanjing Roast Duck

Travel Tips

  • Use metro to visit outer attractions
  • Fall is best time for city walks
  • Visit Qinhuai River area at night

Harbin

Ice and snow wonderland with Russian influence.

Top Attractions

  • Harbin Ice and Snow Festival
  • Saint Sophia Cathedral
  • Sun Island Park
  • Zhongyang Pedestrian Street

Must-Try Foods

  • Harbin Sausages
  • Borscht
  • Ice Cream (even in winter!)

Travel Tips

  • Dress in thermal layers for January
  • Book festival tickets early
  • Try Russian-style cafes

Sanya

Tropical island paradise with stunning beaches.

Top Attractions

  • Yalong Bay
  • Tianya Haijiao
  • Nanshan Temple
  • Wuzhizhou Island

Must-Try Foods

  • Hainan Chicken Rice
  • Coconut Rice
  • Seafood BBQ

Travel Tips

  • Book resorts in advance during holidays
  • Use sunscreen - strong tropical sun
  • Avoid Spring Festival for crowd reasons

Huangshan

Majestic yellow mountains with breathtaking views.

Top Attractions

  • Huangshan Mountain
  • Xihai Grand Canyon
  • Hot Springs
  • Ancient Villages (Hongcun/Xidi)

Must-Try Foods

  • Stinky Tofu
  • Huangshan Maofeng Tea
  • Bamboo Shoots Dishes

Travel Tips

  • Best time is April-November
  • Bring warm clothes for mountain top
  • Consider cable car to save energy

Datong

Ancient Buddhist grottoes and hanging temple.

Top Attractions

  • Yungang Grottoes
  • Hanging Temple
  • Nine Dragon Screen
  • Datong City Wall

Must-Try Foods

  • Datong Knife-Cut Noodles
  • Yellow Rice Cake
  • Lamb Hot Pot

Travel Tips

  • Spring and autumn best seasons
  • Hire local guide for grottoes
  • Combine with Pingyao trip

Kaifeng

Ancient capital with rich Song Dynasty heritage.

Top Attractions

  • Millennium City Park
  • Iron Pagoda
  • Dragon Pavilion
  • Kaifeng Night Market

Must-Try Foods

  • Steamed Buns with Soup
  • Peanut Cakes
  • Spicy Lamb Soup

Travel Tips

  • Visit during Qingming Festival
  • Try night market street food
  • Combine with Zhengzhou trip

Macau

East meets West with Portuguese influence.

Top Attractions

  • Ruins of St. Paul's
  • Macau Tower
  • Senado Square
  • Venetian Macao

Must-Try Foods

  • Portuguese Egg Tarts
  • Pork Chop Bun
  • Almond Cookies

Travel Tips

  • No visa needed for mainland Chinese
  • Use free casino shuttles
  • Visit Coloane for local vibe

Yangshuo

Karst mountains and Li River scenery.

Top Attractions

  • Li River Cruise
  • Moon Hill
  • West Street
  • Bamboo Rafting

Must-Try Foods

  • Beer Fish
  • Guilin Rice Noodles
  • Stuffed Li River Snails

Travel Tips

  • Best seasons are spring and fall
  • Rent bicycle to explore countryside
  • Watch Impression Liu Sanjie show

Dali

Bai minority culture and Erhai Lake.

Top Attractions

  • Erhai Lake
  • Three Pagodas
  • Dali Ancient Town
  • Cangshan Mountain

Must-Try Foods

  • Rushan Cheese
  • Xizhou Baba
  • Erhai Lake Fish

Travel Tips

  • March-May best for flowers
  • Rent electric bike for lake tour
  • Visit Shaxi on way to Lijiang

Qingdao

Coastal city with beer, beaches, and German heritage.

Top Attractions

  • Zhanqiao Pier
  • Qingdao Beer Museum
  • Badaguan Scenic Area
  • Mount Laoshan

Must-Try Foods

  • Tsingtao Beer
  • Grilled Squid
  • Clam Soup

Travel Tips

  • July-August is best for beach trips
  • Try street seafood near night markets
  • Join brewery tours for fun local experience

Zhangjiajie

Inspiration for the Avatar movie's floating mountains.

Top Attractions

  • Zhangjiajie National Forest Park
  • Tianmen Mountain
  • Glass Skywalk
  • Bailong Elevator

Must-Try Foods

  • Tujia Bacon
  • Sour Fish Soup
  • Wild Mushroom Dishes

Travel Tips

  • Prepare for stairs and altitude
  • Avoid foggy days for better mountain views
  • Book hotels near Wulingyuan

Lijiang

Charming old town with ethnic Naxi culture.

Top Attractions

  • Lijiang Old Town (UNESCO)
  • Jade Dragon Snow Mountain
  • Black Dragon Pool
  • Shuhe Ancient Town

Must-Try Foods

  • Naxi Barbecue
  • Yak Butter Tea
  • Er Kuai (Rice Cake)

Travel Tips

  • Altitude may affect some visitors
  • Stay inside Old Town for best experience
  • Visit nearby Yunnan ethnic villages
